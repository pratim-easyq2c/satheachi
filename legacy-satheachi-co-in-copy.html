Frequently Asked Questions

I feel guilty for not being there. How can a service replace a child's presence?

At Sathe Achi, we don't replace you — we support you. You provide the emotional connection through calls and visits, while we take care of practical needs like emergencies, doctor visits, or even picking up fresh fish from the bazaar. This way, your time with your parents can be about love and joy, not chores.

How do I know what's actually happening with my parents?

We keep you informed with clear, regular updates. Whether it's a doctor's report or a photo of them enjoying an activity, you'll always know how they're doing. Think of us as your transparent local bridge.

What happens if there is a midnight emergency?

Emergencies can be stressful, especially from afar. Our Emergency Membership provides 24/7 support, hospital coordination, and immediate presence. You won't need to call neighbors in panic — we'll already be there.

Can you help with essential tasks like banking or groceries?

Yes. Many seniors find crowded markets or online banking difficult. We assist with banking, bazaar trips, and grocery management, so they can stay independent without the strain.

Why should I let outsiders help me when I've managed on my own for years?

True independence means having the freedom to focus on what you enjoy. By letting us handle tasks like medical test queues or household repairs, you save your energy for companionship, hobbies, and social life.

I feel lonely since my children moved away. Do you offer more than medical care?

Absolutely. Life in Kolkata is about Adda and community. Our companionship and activity services bring connection back into daily life, ensuring emotional well-being alongside physical health.

Is the staff reliable and respectful of my home?

Trust is at the heart of Sathe Achi. With over 12 years in Kolkata, our team is trained not only in caregiving but also in the cultural nuances of Bengali households. We treat every home with the respect it deserves.

What if I only need help for a few weeks while my family is on vacation?

We offer flexible Short-Term Care and Special Memberships. Whether it's recovery support or temporary help while relatives are away, we step in seamlessly and step out when you're ready.

    We Make a Difference in Senior Lives

    Since 2014 | Barasat, Kolkata

    The verge world has wrapped itself up inside an abalone. 'Para Culture' is almost gone. Every single family is now separated. The concept of 'parar chhele' or 'the guy next door' is almost extinct.

    There was a time, 20-30 years back, when few young guys from the locality were always there to help and support the elders. 'Parar kaku/jyethu's' were mostly dependent on them. ''Eiii, edike ekbar shune ja to''— was more than enough to gather 5-10 people. There was unity and coherence in every para and among its people.

    But those days are almost over.

    Young people leaving their family behind are anxious about their parents at home. They are well connected with their parents through phone or social media. But surely they do understand that there are times when physical presence is also very important. Like a faithful hand and anchored soul.

    Here the concept of 'SATHE ACHI' emerges.
    We are here to support elders for their medical and non-medical needs.
