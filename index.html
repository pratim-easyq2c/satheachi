Questions

Honest answers

What happens at 2 AM if there's an emergency?

Call our 24/7 line. We coordinate ambulance, hospital admission, documents, and stay with your parent until family arrives — whether you're in Salt Lake or Seattle.

I'm abroad. How do I know what's actually happening?

Your Care Sathi sends regular updates — doctor reports, photos from activities, and direct WhatsApp access. We're your transparent local bridge.

Do you replace family?

Never. We handle logistics so your calls and visits are about connection — not chores, queues, and panic.

Can I try for just a few weeks?

Yes — Short Term Care is built for vacations, recovery, or trial periods. Deposit refunded within 72 hours if you discontinue.

Will caregivers respect our home and customs?

Trust is everything. Our team is trained for Bengali households — shoes off, kitchen rules, the rhythms of your family.
